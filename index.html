iDontStop

Double tap to RECORD

Single tap to PLAY

Hold down to NAME, SHARE, and DOWNLOAD

Replace all sounds

You will need to be connected to the internet in order to SHARE or DOWNLOAD sounds.

Shared sounds are for public use and cannot be copy written.

Sounds that are shared to the server will remain there until the server capacity limit is reached. The oldest sounds will be deleted first.

You cannot preview or choose the sounds you download. It's about serendipity.

If you want better sound quality use headphones or an input into your stereo.
